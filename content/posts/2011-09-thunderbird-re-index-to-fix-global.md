Using Thunderbird on more than one machine can lead to messages missing from the index and reducing the usefulness of the global search.<br />
<br />
Two easy options to re-index your IMAP folders and fix the problem<br />
<br />
1. Right click and open properties for the folder you want to fix, and click repair folder. This will also download the mail again from the server.<br />
<br />

<br />
2.&nbsp;&nbsp;Find and delete all of the files with the .msf file extension in your profile folder, as described <a href="http://forums.mozillazine.org/viewtopic.php?f=39&amp;t=609239&amp;start=0&amp;st=0&amp;sk=t&amp;sd=a">here</a>
