<h2>The Power of AI-Assisted Data Analysis</h2>

<p>How good is AI at helping with data analysis?</p> 

<p>Last year, I explored this question by analysing blog activity in our BC RSS WhatsApp Community -<a href="https://www.linkedin.com/posts/andywingate_easycharts-dataanalysis-chatgpt-activity-7276363817243004928-Lc3u" target="_blank">check out that post here</a>.</p>

<h2>The AI Factor: How This Analysis Was Created 🤖</h2>

<p>Just like last year, all analysis and visualization for this post was done using AI assistance - specifically, this content was generated from a single WhatsApp chat export file. What would have taken hours of Excel manipulation was accomplished through natural language processing and PowerShell scripting.</p>

<p>The data was:</p>
<ol>
    <li><strong>Extracted</strong> from raw WhatsApp chat logs (5,500+ lines)</li>
    <li><strong>Filtered</strong> to exclude Microsoft Learn and focus on community content</li>
    <li><strong>Cleaned</strong> to isolate blog post URLs from Dec 2024 - Nov 2025</li>
    <li><strong>Parsed</strong> into structured CSV format (1,476 posts)</li>
    <li><strong>Analyzed</strong> for patterns, trends, and insights</li>
    <li><strong>Visualized</strong> <em>(charts to be added)</em></li>
</ol>

<p>This is practical AI in action - not replacing expertise, but augmenting our ability to make sense of large datasets quickly.</p>
