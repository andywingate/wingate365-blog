<h2 style="text-align: left;">Introduction</h2>
<p>Joining a Microsoft Partner as a Dynamics 365 Business Central Consultant can be a game-changer for your career. Microsoft provide partners with a wealth of resources and support that can help you advance your skills and enable you to deliver better solutions to your clients. Many partners also reward staff directly for certification passes and certainly the success will be celebrated! Microsoft certifications not only validate your skills but also make you more attractive to potential clients and future employers. Always setup the link between your personal Microsoft account and your work account from the <a href="https://esi.microsoft.com/">Enterprise Skills Initiative: Welcome (microsoft.com)</a> page to secure your achievements for the long term.  Once passed an exam be sure to add a reminder for any renewals for those that expire.
