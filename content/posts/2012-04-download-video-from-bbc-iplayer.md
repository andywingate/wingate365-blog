There is no quick answer to this from Google for Windows, but in Ubuntu it's dead easy<br />
<br />
<code>$ sudo apt-get install get-iplayer&nbsp;</code><br />
<br />
With the package is installed run<br />
<br />
<code>$ get-iplayer --get --url=<any a="" bbc="" on="" page="" video="" with=""></any></code><br />
<br />
