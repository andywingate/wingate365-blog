<code># mount.cifs //Server/Share /mnt/win7/test -o username=xxx,password=xxx<br />
</code>
<br />
<br />
This command will mount the folder 'Share' hosted on 'Server' into the nix folder /mnt/win7/test<br />
<br />
Network locations are case sensitive!<br />
