I'm **Andy Wingate** - Business Central Practice Director at Venture Systems Group and a
Microsoft MVP for Business Central. This blog is my field notebook: notes on Business
Central, Dynamics 365, the Microsoft cloud, and whatever else I'm tinkering with.

## A bit of background

I've worked with Dynamics NAV and Business Central since around 2015. I spent roughly 12
years on the end-user side, latterly running IT and business systems, before moving to
the partner side in 2023. Having sat in both chairs shapes how I write here: practical,
first-hand, and focused on what actually works in a real implementation.

I was recognised as a Microsoft MVP for Business Central in 2025.

## What you'll find here

- Business Central and Dynamics 365 how-tos, fixes, and gotchas
- Microsoft cloud, Power Platform, and Copilot notes
- The occasional homelab or off-topic experiment
